Biographical Note · Music

Flute & Piccolo

I am a former Flute Performance major at the Northwestern University Bienen School of Music. I have played the flute for over seven years, and my orchestral experience includes flute and piccolo roles with the San Francisco Symphony's Youth Orchestra, where I rehearsed up to thirteen hours per week at Davies Symphony Hall and worked with professional musicians from the San Francisco Symphony and SF Conservatory of Music.
