Biographical Note · Music

Flute & Piccolo

I am a former Flute Performance major at the Bienen School of Music at Northwestern University, and have been playing the flute for over seven years. My orchestral experience includes principal flute and piccolo with the San Francisco Symphony Youth Orchestra, where I rehearsed up to thirteen hours per week at Davies Symphony Hall and performed alongside professional musicians from the SF Symphony.
