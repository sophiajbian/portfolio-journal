Led the electrical subteam for a competitive FIRST Robotics team based at NASA Ames Research Center — designing and building full robot systems from scratch each season on an 8-week build cycle.

Electrical Systems CAD SOLIDWORKS Fabrication Team Leadership FRC

1. Overview

The Team

FIRST Robotics Competition Team #1868, Space Cookies, is based at NASA Ames Research Center in Mountain View, CA. Each season, teams have 8 weeks to design, build, and program a robot to compete in a new game revealed each January.

Over three years, I grew from a member on the electrical and mechanical subteams to leading the electrical subteam — responsible for the robot's entire wiring architecture, power distribution, and sensor integration. The team qualified to the FIRST World Championships twice during my time.

2. Electrical

System Architecture

As electrical lead, I was responsible for the full electrical system from power distribution to sensor wiring, ensuring everything was competition-ready and reliable under match conditions.

SubsystemResponsibility

3. Mechanical

Design & Fabrication

  • Designed chassis layouts and mechanical subsystems using top-down CAD modeling in SOLIDWORKS
  • Executed machining, fabrication, and full subsystem integration across mechanical interfaces
  • Managed component positioning and packaging within tight robot frame constraints
  • Collaborated across electrical and mechanical subteams throughout 8-week build cycles
